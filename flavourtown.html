Flavourtowns Restaurant Review

Location: 7 Marais Road Germiston

Contact: 27 (11) 453-0587

Overview

FlavourTown as the name suggest is a plce absolutly filled with tasty dishes. The atmosphere here is also amazing, the staff are lovely to converse with and they do their jobs very well. The decor is also very beutiful and the food looks as good as it tastes.

The Food

The food here is simply wonderful, whether you get the cheesecake, lobster or the lambs. Anything you get will be absolutly lovely and I wholeheartedly reccomend this place for whenever you have the time or money.

Meals at Flavourtown

Lobster: The meal started off strong with the great Lobsters that they have, the meat is soft and tender, the sauce that they use is simply lovely as well. this is the perfect meal for any seafood lover.

Lamb: The lamb is super good, the tenderness of their meat is simply something that has to appreciated, they manage to keep it tender while having such a good chew to them. The lamb shank is something I would reccomend to anyone.

Cheese Cake: The cheese cake here made me appreciate cheese ckaes in general, It is firm but has a lovely softness to it and it is such a joy to eat .
